## Interview Process

### 1. **HR Screening Round**

* **Duration**: ~20–30 minutes
* **Format**: Discussion
* **Questions Asked**:

  * Total professional experience and relevant experience in Rust.
  * Reason for job change.
  * Verification against the job requirements and role expectations.
* **Outcome**:

  * Profile was shortlisted.
  * L1 Technical Interview was scheduled on the **same day at 4 PM**.

---

### 2. **Round 1 – L1 Technical Interview**

* **Duration**: ~45 minutes

* **Format**: Technical discussion + Live coding

* **Questions Asked**:

  * Tell me about yourself.
  * Self-rating in Rust and justification.
  * Number of Rust projects worked on and detailed end-to-end explanation of one project.
  * How Rust eliminates the need for a garbage collector.

    * Discussion on ownership model, borrowing rules, and lifetimes.
  * If Rust allows only one owner at a time, how does it support memory sharing across threads?

    * Explanation of `Rc` for single-threaded and `Arc` for multi-threaded shared ownership.
  * Follow-up implementation question:

    * Implement a counter that can be updated concurrently by multiple variables/threads.

* **Coding Question**:

  * **LeetCode (Medium)** – [Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters](https://leetcode.com/problems/longest-substring-without-repeating-characters/)
  * Approach, edge cases, and time/space complexity were discussed.
  * The solution was implemented successfully.

* **Discussion Points**:

  * Ownership, Borrowing, and Lifetimes
  * Rust concurrency and shared state
  * Rust project experience
  * DSA problem-solving in Rust

---

### 3. **Round 2 – Client Technical Interview**

* **Duration**: ~30 minutes

* **Format**: Technical discussion

* **Questions Asked**:

  * Detailed discussion on previous work experience based on my resume.
  * What is blockchain, why it is required, and why it is considered secure.
  * How to efficiently render a very large list in React.
  * Why Rust is better compared to other programming languages.
  * What are the overheads or trade-offs when using Rust.
  * Reason for transitioning from frontend development to Rust.

* **Discussion Points**:

  * System design thinking
  * Blockchain fundamentals
  * Frontend performance optimization
  * Language trade-offs and career transition rationale
